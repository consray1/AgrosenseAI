🤖 AI Farm Advisor

Ask anything.
Get farm-ready answers.

The AI Advisor understands crop growth stages, local hazards, soil conditions, and market context — and answers in the farmer's language.

1
Farmer registers crop & location
One-time setup via WhatsApp, USSD, or the app. Name, crop type, county, planting date.
2
HUSIKA alert fires
A drought or flood warning triggers the AgroSense pipeline automatically.
3
AI generates personalized advice
RAG retrieves agronomic guidance. GPT-4o generates 3 crop-specific actions in 8 seconds.
4
Farmer receives it on their phone
WhatsApp for smartphones. USSD or SMS for feature phones. No internet needed.
